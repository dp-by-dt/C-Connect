About C-Connect

Building stronger campus communities, one connection at a time

Our Mission

C-Connect was created to solve a simple problem: connecting students on campus. In a world dominated by global social media, we saw the need for a platform that brings together the people you actually interact with daily.

We believe meaningful connections happen locally. By focusing on your campus, we help you discover study partners, find event buddies, join interest groups, and build friendships that matter.
