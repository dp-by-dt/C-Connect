About C-Connect

We're on a mission to transform how students connect, collaborate, and build meaningful relationships on campus.

Our Mission

C-Connect was created to solve a simple problem: it's hard to meet new people on a large campus. We believe that every student deserves to find their community, discover opportunities, and build connections that last beyond graduation. Our platform makes it easy to find like-minded peers, join interest groups, and stay engaged with campus life.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Privacy First

Your data is yours. We prioritize your privacy and security in everything we build.

Authentic Connections

We foster genuine relationships, not just follower counts. Quality over quantity.

Inclusive Community

Everyone belongs here. We celebrate diversity and create spaces where all students thrive.

Innovation

We continuously improve and adapt to serve our community better.

Student-Centered

Built by students, for students. Your feedback shapes our roadmap.

Growth

We help you grow personally and professionally through meaningful connections.

Made with ❤️ for Students

C-Connect is a passion project created by students who understand the challenges of making connections on campus. We're committed to building the best social platform for students, by students.
